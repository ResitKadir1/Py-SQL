*1-Use inventory.product to explore the two_three olive oil product data

*2-Write query with window function that expands on the information for each product

*3-Display the min max,and avarage price for each product, as well as the total count of products within the same category and ssize classification

*Remeber to use a window clause when frames use the same parameters






select category_id, product_name, size, price,
	max(price) over(w),
	min(price) over(w),
	avg(price) over(w),
	count(*) over(w)
from inventory.products
window w as (partition by category_id, size)
order by category_id, product_name, size;
