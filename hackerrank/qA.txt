Q-6:Query the list of CITY names starting with vowels (i.e., a, e, i, o, or u) from STATION. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

Input Format

The STATION table is described as follows:


A-6:--> 

SELECT DISTINCT(CITY)
FROM STATION
WHERE SUBSTR(CITY,1,1) IN ('A','E','I','O','U');



Q-7--> Query the list of CITY names ending with vowels (a, e, i, o, u) from STATION. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

A-7--> 
select distinct(city)
from station
where substr(city,(length(city)),length(city)) in ('A','E','I','o','u');


Q-8) Query the list of CITY names from STATION which have vowels (i.e., a, e, i, o, and u) as both their first and last characters. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

A-8) 
select distinct(city)
from station
where left(city,1) in ('a','e','i','o','u') and right(city,1) in ('a','e','i','o','u');


Q-9--> Query the list of CITY names from STATION that do not start with vowels. Your result cannot contain duplicates.
A-9--> 

select distinct(city)
from station
where left(city,1) not in ('a','e','i','u','o');


Q-10--> Query the list of CITY names from STATION that do not end with vowels. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

A-10--> 
select distinct(city)
from station
where right(city,1) not in('a','e','i','o','u');


Q-12--> Query the list of CITY names from STATION that do not start with vowels and do not end with vowels. Your result cannot contain duplicates.

A-12 --> 
SELECT DISTINCT(CITY)
FROM STATION
WHERE LEFT(CITY, 1) NOT IN ('A', 'E', 'I', 'O', 'U') AND 
RIGHT(CITY, 1) NOT IN ('A', 'E', 'I', 'O', 'U');
